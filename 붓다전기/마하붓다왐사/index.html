    • 원본 : Mahā-buddha-vaṁsa in the Myanmar language by Ven. Mingun Sayadaw(Bhaddanta Vicittasārābhivaṁsa), 1956-1969.
    • 영역본 : The Great Chronicles of the Buddhas by Mingun Sayādaw, translated into English by T. U Ko Lay, U Tin Lwin and U Tin Oo, 1990.
